About me

My name is Valentina Riscala and Im a professional wedding planner. I have a degree in Interior Design and Equipment. I also took marketing and personal image consulting courses. This company was founded in 1995 for the purpose of creatign bespoke weddings for couples who want their day to be personal, unforgettable and one of a kind. The day before the wedding, or on the wedding day, I will meet you to go through the practicalities and final details for your wedding. On the wedding day I will be present to make sure everything runs smoothly and according to plan. I can help you! Let me assist you in finding the best locations, select the best vendors, give you great ideas and help you with all the little details that are so important.
